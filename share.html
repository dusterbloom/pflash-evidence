Speed-blocked: the FlashPrefill BSA kernel rejects non-Qwen3-0.6B head/layer geometry. The fallback path is 4× slower than baseline. Cross-family is loader-ready but not speed-ready until the BSA kernel template is generalized (multi-week CUDA engineering work).

What works, what does not

24 GB VMM crash (historical): pflash_skip_park works on RTX 3090 (24 GB) WITH ee7 enabled per commit 62bfb11 — no cuMemSetAccess NOT_READY. The historical 24 GB crash was triggered by full-28-layer drafter VRAM footprint, not a hardware limit. Legacy workaround (full-layer drafter): GGML_CUDA_NO_VMM=1 or --pflash-skip-park=false.
VT task excluded: Synthetic VT harness has a filler design confound. Results are not reported.
