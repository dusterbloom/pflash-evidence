Three findings developers need to know

  1. Always use pflash — biggest single contributor across the full spectrum (13× prefill + 47% decode TPS)
  2. dflash without pflash is a net loss — drafter overhead with full KV beats amortization; NIAH 1/5 vs baseline 5/5
  3. Composition = pflash + insurance for long-ctx multi-token decode; the ~25% decode penalty vs pflash-only is the cost of that coverage

Honest caveats

Quality (LongBench HotpotQA): compressed F1 = 0.587 / uncompressed F1 = 0.547 — both inside the published 55–65% range for this Q4_K_M+tq3_0 stack. See full evidence for per-context breakdown.

MED-tier real-session: composition lost 19% tok/J vs pflash-only (Axis D); EMA-driven C2 gate v2 is a candidate follow-up.
